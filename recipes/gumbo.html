Gumbo

Gumbo, one of Louisiana's most favored and most authentic dish. Gumbo can vary from many different ingredients from plain old gravy and rice, to a full fledge gumbo with seafoods of all kind plus chicken. Today it's about meeting right in the middle. Gumbo with shrimp and sausage which is one of the easiest and equally great ways to do this when you want that special taste and authentic dish.

Below we have a list of ingredients that'll make this dish easy to make and just as savory.

Ingredients:

  • roux
  • sausage
  • shrimp
  • rice
  • red pepper
  • black pepper
  • onion
  • bell pepper

Steps:

  1. season shrimp and sausage
  2. dice whole onion and bell pepper
  3. add water to gumbo pot and get to rolling boil
  4. brown shrimp, sausage, onions and bell peppers
  5. after browning set aside ingredients
  6. add roux to boiling water
  7. add shrimp, sausage, onions, bell peppers to pot
  8. cook for one hour to one half hour
